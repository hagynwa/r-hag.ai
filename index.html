My Story

From Infantry Commander to AI Leader

From commanding infantry soldiers in the Givati Brigade to leading AI development at Israel's second-largest health fund — my path has always been about leading through complexity.

I studied Industrial Engineering at the Technion, optimized supply chains for my thesis, then spent years in consulting and project management at Applied Materials and Moore MC. In 2023, I joined Maccabi Health Services to do what I love most: build AI systems that actually ship.

On the side, I teach MBA students, lead the AI First Israel community, and build personal AI projects that solve problems for my family and neighbors.
